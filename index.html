Coffee Milkshake

A coffee milkshake is a delightful blend of coffee, ice cream, and milk, resulting in a rich, creamy, and refreshing drink. It offers the caffeine kick of coffee combined with the sweet indulgence of a milkshake, making it a perfect pick-me-up for any time of day.

Chocolate Cake

Chocolate cake is a classic dessert that is loved by people of all ages. It is a rich, moist cake that is typically made with chocolate, flour, sugar, eggs, butter, and milk. There are many different variations of chocolate cake, but they all share the same basic ingredients and method of preparation.

Ice Cream

Ice cream is a frozen dessert typically made with dairy products, such as milk and cream, sugar, and flavorings. It is usually churned to incorporate air and cooled below the freezing point of water to prevent the formation of large ice crystals. Ice cream can be flavored with a wide variety of ingredients, such as fruits, nuts, chocolates, and candies.

Leche Flan

Leche flan is a Filipino dessert made with eggs, milk, and sugar. It is a custard that is steamed and then chilled until firm. Leche flan is typically served with a caramel sauce.
